About Us

Details of us and our products are given in brief.

Streamlining Rowing Logistics and Club Management with Precision.

Our CEO as a rower himself kept on coming to regattas and heard that certain things for some boats was missing. This was something that often turned out to start problems with available boats at regattas and created stress on the rowers. Also, when loading the trailers for big regattas, it was hard to keep track of what was already on the trailer or not, which lead to such problems. This inspired him to make Rowgistic, a digital solution which helps logistical problems within clubs. From this original idea, we expanded on this and thought, why not make an all-in-one app for rowing. This led to the app we offer today.

The Rowgistic App is an all-in-one app for rowers. The app provides services for every functional part required for a club to organize trainings and regattas. The app easily let you provide important information to the club members and make it easily accessible. Maybe most importantly, it solves the original problem. This is done through our quality check, which makes the crew of the boat check off the condition of different parts of the boat, to make sure everything is in good condition and loaded on the trailer among other things.
